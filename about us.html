About LanuCi

Welcome to LanuCi, a high-end fashion brand offering premium styles designed for every occasion. Our collections are carefully curated to provide the latest trends while maintaining comfort and elegance. We believe in fashion that empowers and inspires.

Our Mission

To celebrate cultural heritage through wearable art, embracing diversity, and reviving vintage styles with a modern twist.

Our Vision

Bridging cultural heritage and modern artistry, our brand creates fashion that celebrates diversity, self-expression, and timeless elegance.

Founders

LanuCi was founded by Saige Cielo and her husband Leonardo Cielo
